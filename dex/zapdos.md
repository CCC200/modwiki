# OU
Zapdos is the premiere rain setter of the entire format and a massive offensive threat in its own right. With access to both STAB Thunder and Hurricane alongside its new ability Drizzle, Zap not only enables rain synergy but can directly use it. Paired with healing via Roost and utility options like Thunder Wave, U-Turn or Volt Switch, it's clear this bird will be top-tier for as long as it's around.

-- Standard Offensive Set
Zapdos @ Damp Rock  
Ability: Drizzle  
Tera Type: Electric  
EVs: 4 HP / 252 Def / 252 Spe  
Bold Nature  
IVs: 15 HP / 0 Atk / 15 Def / 15 SpA / 15 SpD / 15 Spe  
- Thunder  
- Hurricane  
- Volt Switch  
- Roost
--

This set is your bread and butter, made to apply pressure from turn 1. Offensive investment is not even required as the base power of its attacking moves are so high, and it would prefer defense to reliably reset rain and heal off chip via Roost. Choice Scarf and Leftovers are also options with their own tradeoffs. Truthfully, this set is powerful enough you probably don't even need to use it on a rain team; it could be slot in on many existing structures.
